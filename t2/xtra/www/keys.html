Keyboard commands:


CURSOR / NUMPAD KEYS - move around the dungeon
SPACE - pause and wait
C - Chat with friendly being
D - Drop item
E - Eat something
I - View inventory
K - Kick something
L - Look at square
O - Open / close door
P - Pick up item
Q - Quaff potion
R - Read book or scroll
S - Search for hidden traps and doors
T - Throw item
U - Use an item
V - View stats
W - Wield weapon / wear armour / put on ring
X - Exit dungeon / use staircase / enter or leave location
Z - Cast spell
